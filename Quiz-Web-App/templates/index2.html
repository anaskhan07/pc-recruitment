What we do

Students participating in the activities of Programmers Club get multiple benefits as following:

Workshops

PC organises workshops based on different programming languages, different development tools, etc. which helps students to learn things that are not included in academics.

Events

PC organises college level events such as Hackathons,etc. which gives students an opportunity to showcase the skills they already know or the skills they learnt in our workshops.

Algorithm

Our highlight event is ALGORTHM, which is organised at national level. Students from different colleges of different cities of different states participate in this 36-hour long event. They are given a problem to solve, team with best solution is awarded accordingly. Also, students can get benefits like internships and job opportunities,etc.

Upskilling

Students who get reqruited in Programmers Club as core members, upgrade their skills and also learn new things apart frim academic studies.

Confidence Boost

Students who get reqruited in Programmers Club as core members, get their confidence boosted up by teaching students in the club as well as by teaching in workshops, which helps them in interviews, presentations,etc.

Management Skills

By organising and managing multiple events including a national level event, members develop their Management skills which helps them later in their corporate life.
